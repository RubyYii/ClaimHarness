# Political Theory Education Risk Alignment

I want to evaluate LLM hallucination, ideological bias, and value risk in political theory education. The problem is not ordinary factual QA. The model must stay source-grounded, preserve concept relations, avoid unsupported claims, and avoid unstable value framing.

The risk is that an AI practitioner evaluates only answer fluency or factual accuracy. A fluent answer can still misstate a curriculum concept, selectively frame an issue, or omit uncertainty and source grounding. Teacher review must remain part of the workflow.
